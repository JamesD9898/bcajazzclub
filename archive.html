The Club

The Bergen County Academies Jazz Club is a completely student-run program, dedicated to learning and performing jazz together.

The Jazz Club is the culmination of our collective efforts to get more exposure to older tunes of composers like Duke Ellington and Dizzy Gilespie and more modern takes on swing as well.

We’ve had a successful debut year in the 2023-2024 school year, performing in the Essentially Ellington Regional Festival at Newark Academy, and we’re thrilled to continue the club next year.

What we're about

Repertoire

We make sure to learn repertoire almost every lesson. So far with this music we've done great things. After participating in the Essentially Ellington Regional festival, we had 2 soloists, James Doherty (alto sax) and David Villarosa (trumpet), win outstanding soloist awards. We also received tickets to go watch the national Essentially Ellington final concert at Jazz at Lincoln Center for free.

Improvisation

Jazz isn’t jazz without improvisation, but learning how to improvise can be tough.
That’s why we have specific meetings, where we give presentations on music theory, working our way up to improvisation.
Our lessons have so far been about intervals, building chords, common chord progressions, modes, and rhythm for 8 bar blues.

Meetings

Meetings are always a blast and can range in topics. Many of our meetings comprise of learning and playing through Jazz Repertoire which we perform at local venues and festivals. However, most excitingly, we learn Jazz techniques on some basic theory, improvisation, practicing and expression. Lessons are always interactive and instruments are always involved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the BCA Jazz Club?

Anyone who has a basic understanding of one of the following instrument:

Saxophone, Trumpet, Trombone, Clarinet, Piano, Bass, Guitar or Drums.

You don't have to be a virtuoso or know about jazz to join the club! If you're a jazz master or just looking to try something new, all are welcome as long as your comfortable with playing some music!

How can I join the BCA Jazz Club?

The BCA Jazz Club runs during the designated Clubs Period on Wednesdays (Period 9). If your new to BCA, you'll be able to sign up for the club during the beginning of every trimester.

However, you can get in touch now! Fill out this Form to join the mailing list! We'll keep in touch as we have some events being planned for this summer!

What are you guys up to?

The BCA Jazz Club is planning some pretty awesome things this upcoming year!

We plan on revisiting the Essentially Ellington Regional festival, perform at local venues such as churches, school events, and more to come.
